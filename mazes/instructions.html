Instructions

You will be given three mazes of size 10x10. These mazes will not be visible. You will always start from the top and the end will always be at the bottom of the maze. For each maze you will be given either a visual, auditory, or no cue to indicate if you have moved into a wall or an empty space.

To begin solving a maze please press the "begin" button. You must then solve the maze before being able to move on to the next one. If you feel like it is impossible to solve the maze, you can select "Give Up".

After completing all three maze you will be given a short survey.
